About

SVATANTR

SVATANTR is a blockchain backed SCM solution designed for secure and managed transport of liquor from distillery to the liquor permit holders in various states of India According to Reserve Bank of India, the state excise duty on alcohol is the second largest contributor to a state’s own tax revenue (following sales tax- now replaced by GST), accounting to about 10 to 15 percent for the majority of the states

SVATANTR helps in improving accountablity and audit trails. We also help in preventing Counterfiet products entering the markets and lack of visibility.
